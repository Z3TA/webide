Enabling new windows (aka popups)

Most web browsers will block new windows by default.
When for example previewing a web document, the editor wants to open it in a new window (which will be blocked by the browser).

How to enable new windows (also known as "popups") ....

Chome/Opera/Edge

  1. Click on the icon/widget left of the domain in the address-field
  2. Click on "site settings".
  3. Right to "Pop-ups and redirects" select Allow.

Firefox

  1. Click on the icon/widget left of the domain in the address-field, it usually looks like a pad-lock.
  2. Click on the right arrow right to the connection status.
  3. Click on "more information"
  4. Choose the permissions tab
  5. Under "Open Pop-up Windows" deselect "use default" and choose "Allow"
